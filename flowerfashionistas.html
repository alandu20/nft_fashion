Creators

Consume-to-Earn: Get rewarded for your attention

Consumers

Our Vision is to create an ecosystem facilitating symbiotic growth between sustainable fashion brands, content creators, and everyday consumers. Learn more about how the ecosystem works below.


Sustainable Fashion Brands have an opportunity to grow their brand by offering rewards to creators who produce content that promotes the brand to consumers, as well as their loyal followers.


Content Creators can build their personal brand while also earning rewards from the sustainable brands they support. Rewards are distributed based on the amount of interaction with their content.


Consumers not only follow their favorite creators but also earn for participating in the ecosystem. No social media platform exists without their users, so we believe they should be rewarded too.

02Benefits of Ownership
Governance
Shape the Future

Each holder is entitled to a vote on topics such as which brands qualify as "sustainable" and what sustainability-driven non-profits will the future FF Fund donate a portion of profits to. Participation in each decision will be commemorated with official POAPs.

Ownership
Full Ownership of Artwork and IP

The artwork will be solely owned by the holder. The metasphere is the limit!

FF Club
Own a Stake

A set of extremely rare FFs will recieve a portion of any remaining profits after ecosystem rewards and FF Fund donations are paid out.
